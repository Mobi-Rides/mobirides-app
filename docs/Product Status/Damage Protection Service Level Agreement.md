## 2
- Scope of Services
2.1 Platform Responsibilities (MobiRides)
- Provide the digital marketplace and booking infrastructure
- Collect premiums from Renters at the point of booking
- Administer first-line claims intake, evidence collection, and triage
- Maintain risk scoring models and fraud detection
- Remit collected premiums to the Underwriter per the agreed schedule
- Provide monthly reporting on attach rates, claim volumes, and financials
- Immediately notify the Underwriter (Pay-U) of every policy issued upon successful
purchase of damage protection coverage.
2.2 Underwriter Responsibilities (Pay-U)
- Provide licensed insurance backing for all damage protection tiers
- Underwrite policies issued through the Platform
- Process and adjudicate escalated claims
- Settle approved claims within agreed SLA timelines
- Provide coverage certificates and policy documentation
- Maintain regulatory compliance in all operating jurisdictions

## 3.3 Coverage Inclusions
All paid tiers include:
- Accidental collision damage
- Single-vehicle incidents
- Weather damage (hail, flooding, storm)
- Vandalism and malicious damage
- Attempted theft damage
## 3.4 Coverage Exclusions
All tiers exclude:
- Intentional or reckless damage
- Driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs
- Unlicensed or unauthorized drivers
- Oc-road use (unless vehicle is rated for oc-road)
- Mechanical wear and tear

## 8. Risk Management
## 8.1 Risk Scoring Tiers
## Risk Level Multiplier Criteria
Low 1.0x Verified renter, good history, standard vehicle
Medium 1.1x New renter, moderate vehicle value
High 1.3x Previous claims, high-value vehicle
Prohibited N/A — coverage declined Fraud history, suspended account

## 8.2 Fraud Prevention
- Platform maintains automated fraud detection on all claims
- Suspicious claims flagged for manual review before forwarding to Pay-U
- Shared fraud intelligence between parties (anonymized)
- Joint investigation process for claims exceeding P20,000




## 12
## 9. Liability & Indemnification
## 9.1 Coverage Cap Adherence
- No single claim payout shall exceed the tier coverage cap
- Aggregate annual liability cap to be agreed separately per coverage year
- Pay-U bears no liability for claims on bookings without active policies
## 9.2 Platform Liability
MobiRides shall:
- Ensure accurate premium collection and timely remittance
- Maintain accurate records of all policies issued
- Not misrepresent coverage terms to Renters or Hosts
- Indemnify Pay-U against losses arising from Platform negligence in claims
administration
## 9.3 Underwriter Liability
Pay-U shall:
- Honor all valid claims within coverage caps and terms
- Maintain adequate reserves for outstanding claims
- Indemnify MobiRides against losses arising from wrongful claim denial
