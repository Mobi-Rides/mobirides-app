# Beta Pilot Launch Plan

## 1. Executive Summary
This Beta Pilot Launch Plan outlines the strategy for deploying Mobi Rides' ride-hailing service to 50 select users in Gaborone. The pilot will validate our technology, operational processes, and customer experience before full-scale launch.

## 2. Pilot Objectives
- Validate app stability and performance with real users
- Test driver-passenger matching algorithms
- Gather user feedback on features and UX
- Establish operational workflows in Gabor one
- Identify and mitigate risks before full launch

## 3. Participant Selection Criteria

### Primary Criteria:
- **Location**: Must reside or work in Gaborone
- **Age:** 21-55 years old
- **Smartphone:** Android 8.0+ or iOS 12+ with data plan
- **Transportation Needs:** Regular need for taxi/ride-hailing services
- **Tech Savviness:** Comfortable with mobile apps and digital payments

### Selection Process:
1. Application via dedicated beta sign-up form
2. Screening questionnaire to assess eligibility
3. Background check (driver partners only)
4. Random selection from qualified pool to ensure diversity
5. Notification and onboarding session

### Target Participant Mix:
- 60% regular commuters (work/school)
- 25% occasional users (shopping/social)
- 15% corporate users (business travel)
- Balanced gender representation
- Mix of age groups within criteria

## 4. Gaborone Operational Boundaries

### Geographic Scope:
**Primary Service Area:**
- City of Gaborone municipal boundaries
- Extensions: Phakalane, Fairgrounds, Broadhurst, Tlokweng
- Key zones: Central Business District, Government Enclave, Main Mall, Riverwalk Mall

**Coverage Radius:**
- Initial pickup/dropoff within 15km of CBD
- Future expansion based on demand

### Operational Constraints:
- Operating hours: 5:00 AM - 10:00 PM daily
- Peak hours: 6:00-9:00 AM, 4:00-7:00 PM (surge pricing may apply)
- Weather-related restrictions: Heavy rain may suspend service

### Driver Partner Requirements:
- Must possess valid Botswana driver's license
- Vehicle registration and insurance in driver's name
- Vehicle age ≤ 8 years
- Background check clearance
- Completion of training program

## 5. Success Metrics

### Quantitative Metrics:
- **User Adoption:** 85% of invited users complete first ride
- **Ride Volume:** 300+ completed rides during pilot
- **App Performance:** <2% crash rate, <3 second response time
- **Driver Availability:** Average ETA <15 minutes
- **Customer Satisfaction:** NPS >50

### Qualitative Metrics:
- User feedback on app usability (weekly surveys)
- Driver feedback on platform functionality
- Identification of critical bugs/issues
- Feature requests and prioritization

### Business Metrics:
- Cost per ride within 15% of target
- Driver earnings meet minimum threshold
- Payment system reliability (99%+ success rate)

## 6. Risk Mitigation Strategies

### Technical Risks:
- **Risk:** App crashes or performance issues
  **Mitigation:** Dedicated support team, daily monitoring, hotfix capability
- **Risk:** Payment processing failures
  **Mitigation:** Multiple payment providers, cash backup option

### Operational Risks:
- **Risk:** Driver shortages during peak hours
  **Mitigation:** Incentive structure, flexible scheduling
- **Risk:** Safety incidents
  **Mitigation:** Emergency button, driver background checks, real-time tracking

### User Adoption Risks:
- **Risk:** Low participation rate
  **Mitigation:** Incentive program (ride credits), referral bonuses
- **Risk:** Negative feedback
  **Mitigation:** Rapid response team, weekly improvements

### External Risks:
- **Risk:** Regulatory changes
  **Mitigation:** Government relations liaison, compliance monitoring
- **Risk:** Competitor actions
  **Mitigation:** Unique value proposition focus

## 7. Timeline & Milestones

**Week 1:** Participant recruitment and onboarding
**Week 2:** Training and system testing
**Week 3-5:** Pilot execution (data collection)
**Week 6:** Analysis and reporting
**Week 7:** Post-pilot review and full launch planning

## 8. Communication Plan

### Internal:
- Daily standups with operations team
- Weekly executive updates
- Real-time incident communication channel

### External:
- Weekly email updates to participants
- Dedicated support hotline
- In-app notifications for critical information

## 9. Resources Required

### Team:
- Operations Manager
- Technical Support (2)
- Driver Relations (1)
- Data Analyst (part-time)

### Budget:
- Participant incentives: $1,500
- Marketing/promotion: $500
- Contingency: $1,000
- Total: $3,000

## 10. Conclusion
This beta pilot provides a controlled environment to validate our business model and technology. With clear criteria, defined boundaries, and comprehensive metrics, we will gather actionable insights to ensure a successful full-scale launch in Gaborone and beyond.
