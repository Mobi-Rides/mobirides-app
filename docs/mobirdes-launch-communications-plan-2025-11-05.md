**Date:** November 5, 2025

## Campaign Overview

### Phase 1: Renter Campaign (Week 1-2)
**Target Audience:** People looking to rent vehicles  
**Key Incentive:** **P100 OFF first rental**  
**Primary CTA:** Register & Book Your First Ride  
**Focus Features:** Car catalog, search/filters, booking process, payment

### Phase 2: Host Campaign (Week 3-4)
**Target Audience:** Car owners looking to earn income  
**Key Incentive:** TBD (e.g., "Earn up to P5,000/month")  
**Primary CTA:** List Your Car & Start Earning

## Technical Guidelines

### Screenshot Specifications
- **Device:** iPhone 13/14 (standard size) or equivalent Android
- **Resolution:** 1170 x 2532 px minimum
- **Format:** PNG (for clarity) or JPG (for file size)
- **Orientation:** Portrait (9:16 aspect ratio preferred)
- **Clean UI:** Remove personal info, use demo accounts
- **Status Bar:** Show full battery, good signal (looks professional)
- **Time:** Set to 9:41 AM (iOS standard)

### Screenshot Editing
- **Tool:** Figma, Canva, or Adobe Express
- **Annotations:** 
  - Use arrows/circles to highlight key features
  - Brand colors: [Insert MobiRides brand colors]
  - Font: Clean sans-serif (Poppins, Inter, or similar)
- **Borders:** Add subtle phone frame mockup for social posts

### Video Recording Specifications
- **Resolution:** 1080p minimum (1920 x 1080)
- **Frame Rate:** 30 fps or 60 fps
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Orientation:** Portrait for Reels/TikTok, Square for Feed
- **Length:** 
  - Reels/TikTok: 15-60 seconds
  - Feed: 30-90 seconds max
- **Audio:** Background music + text overlays (no voiceover needed)

### Screen Recording Tools
- **iOS:** Built-in Screen Recording (Settings > Control Center)
- **Android:** AZ Screen Recorder, Mobizen
- **Editing:** CapCut, InShot, or Adobe Premiere Rush

## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Issues & Solutions

**Issue:** Low engagement on early posts  
**Solution:** Have backup content ready to pivot messaging

**Issue:** Technical bugs discovered mid-campaign  
**Solution:** Focus messaging on working features only, acknowledge bugs transparently

**Issue:** Negative comments/reviews
