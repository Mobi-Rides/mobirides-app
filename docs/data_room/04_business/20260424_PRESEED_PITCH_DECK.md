## 🎯 The Problem

Botswana's transportation is broken:
- **Limited options:** Few reliable, affordable taxi services
- **Inefficient:** Long wait times, poor routing, high costs
- **Cash-based:** No digital payments, lack of transparency
- **No sharing economy:** Car owners can't monetize idle assets

---

## 🚀 Our Solution

MobiRides is Botswana's first peer-to-peer car-sharing and Rent2Buy marketplace.

### How it works:
1. **Owners** list their vehicles on the platform
2. **Renters** book rides via app (web & mobile)
3. **Seamless handover** with digital verification and damage documentation
4. **Integrated payments** with wallet system and insurance

### Key Features:
- **7-Step Digital Handover** — secure, verified vehicle exchange
- **Real-time Messaging** — encrypted communication
- **Insurance Integration** — Pay-U damage protection
- **Dynamic Pricing** — market-based rates
- **Rent2Buy** — path to ownership

## 💰 Business Model

### Revenue Streams:
1. **Commission (15%)** on each booking
2. **Insurance attach** — damage protection fees
3. **Wallet top-ups** — transaction margins
4. **Rent2Buy** — transaction fees on sales

### Unit Economics:
- **Revenue per booking:** P168.75 (standardized)
- **Gross margin:** 24.5%
- **Host LTV:CAC:** 60.75:1 (exceptional)
- **Renter LTV:CAC:** 4.05:1
