## 🚀 The Opportunity

Botswana's transportation market is broken. With limited options, inefficient services, and no digital payments, the country needs a modern solution. MobiRides is Botswana's first peer-to-peer car-sharing and Rent2Buy marketplace, connecting car owners with renters through a trusted, technology-enabled platform.

### Why MobiRides?
- **First-mover advantage** in Botswana's P2P car-sharing market
- **Two-product strategy**: P2P car sharing (live) + Rent2Buy marketplace (Q2 2026 pilot)
- **Exceptional traction**: 247 registered users, 66 active vehicles, P311K revenue (+305% YoY)
- **Validated unit economics**: Host LTV:CAC 60.75:1, Renter LTV:CAC 4.05:1
- **Strong partnerships**: Pay-U (Damage Protection), Trillo Rentals (fleet), DPO/Paygate (payments)

## 🌍 Market Opportunity

### Total Addressable Market:
- **Botswana car rental market:** P250M+ annually
- **SADC population:** 380M+ across 16 member states
- **Urban population (Botswana):** ~1.8M people
- **Vehicle ownership:** <20% in Botswana — massive unmet demand

### Market Validation:
- **No direct competitors** in Botswana P2P car-sharing
- **First-mover advantage** in SADC region
- **Anchor partnership:** Trillo fleet (expanded to 3 locations)
- **Insurance partnership:** Pay-U integrated

---

## 💰 Business Model

### Revenue Streams:
1. **Commission (15%)** on each booking
2. **Insurance attach** — damage protection fees
3. **Wallet top-ups** — transaction margins
4. **Rent2Buy** — transaction fees on sales

### Unit Economics:
- **Revenue per booking:** P168.75 (standardized)
- **Gross margin:** 24.5%
- **Host LTV:CAC:** 60.75:1 (exceptional)
- **Renter LTV:CAC:** 4.05:1
