# MobiRides Insurance & Protection Terms

**Last Updated:** May 8, 2026  
**Version:** 1.2

At MobiRides, safety and peace of mind are our top priorities. This document outlines the insurance protection provided during a MobiRides booking and the responsibilities of both Hosts and Renters.

---

## 1. Scope of Protection
MobiRides facilitates supplemental insurance coverage for all confirmed bookings. This protection is active from the documented "Check-In" time until the documented "Check-Out" time.

## 2. Renter Protection Plans
Renters must select a protection plan during the booking process. The plan determines the Renter’s maximum financial liability (the "Excess") in the event of an accident or damage.

### 2.1 No Coverage
*   **Excess**: Full Liability
*   **Coverage**: None
*   **Ideal for**: Budget-conscious or risk-tolerant renters.

### 2.2 Basic Plan
*   **Coverage Cap**: P8,000
*   **Excess**: 20% of approved claim
*   **Coverage**: Standard third-party liability, collision damage, windscreen and window damage, and tyre protection.
*   **Ideal for**: Short-term city rentals.

### 2.3 Standard Plan
*   **Coverage Cap**: P20,000
*   **Excess**: 15% of approved claim
*   **Coverage**: All Basic features plus accidental collision, theft, vandalism, and fire/weather-related damage.
*   **Ideal for**: Multi-day or intercity trips.

### 2.4 Premium Plan
*   **Coverage Cap**: P50,000
*   **Excess**: 10% of approved claim
*   **Coverage**: Maximum protection including all Standard features.
*   **Ideal for**: High-value vehicles or long-term rentals.

## 3. Important Exclusions
The insurance protection will be **VOID**, and the Renter will be liable for the full cost of all damages and third-party claims if:
*   **Unauthorized Driver**: The vehicle was driven by someone not registered on the MobiRides booking.
*   **Under the Influence**: The driver was under the influence of alcohol, drugs, or impairing medication.
*   **Off-Roading**: The damage occurred while driving on unpaved roads or terrain not suitable for the vehicle.
*   **Gross Negligence**: The damage was caused by racing, stunts, or intentional misuse.
*   **Failure to Report**: The incident was not reported to the Police and MobiRides within the required 2-hour window.

## 4. Claims Process
In the event of an incident:
1.  **Police Report**: A police report is mandatory for all claims involving third parties, theft, or significant damage.
2.  **Documentation**: Photos of the scene, all vehicles involved, and IDs of third parties must be collected.
3.  **Submission**: Submit a claim via the MobiRides app within **24 hours** of the incident.
4.  **Assessment**: An independent assessor appointed by our insurance partner will evaluate the damage and determine repair costs.

## 5. Host Protection
MobiRides protects Hosts against physical damage to their vehicle during a booking, subject to a deductible. Hosts are required to maintain their own primary third-party insurance as per Botswana's **Motor Vehicle Accident (MVA) Fund** requirements.

## 6. Dispute Resolution
Decisions regarding insurance coverage and liability are made by the insurance underwriter based on the evidence provided. MobiRides facilitates this process but does not make final liability determinations.
