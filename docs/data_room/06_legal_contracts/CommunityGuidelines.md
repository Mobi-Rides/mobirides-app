# MobiRides Community Guidelines
**Last updated:** May 8, 2026  
**Version:** 1.1

At MobiRides, we are building a community based on trust, safety, and mutual respect. These Community Guidelines ("Guidelines") outline the standards of behavior expected from all Users (Hosts and Renters) on our Platform. By joining MobiRides, you agree to uphold these values.

---

## 1. Respect and Inclusivity
### 1.1 Zero Tolerance for Discrimination
MobiRides is an inclusive community. We have zero tolerance for discrimination based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, age, disability, sexual orientation, or any other protected characteristic. 

### 1.2 Professional Communication
Maintain a polite and professional tone in all interactions. Harassment, threats, hate speech, or abusive language will result in immediate and permanent removal from the Platform.

## 2. Safety and Trust
### 2.1 Truthfulness
Always provide accurate and complete information on your profile and listings. Misrepresenting yourself, your driving history, or your vehicle's condition undermines the safety of the community.

### 2.2 Privacy
Respect the privacy of other Users. Do not share personal contact information, home addresses, or private conversations outside of the Platform without explicit consent.

### 2.3 Off-Platform Transactions
To protect your security and insurance coverage, all bookings and payments must stay on the MobiRides Platform. Soliciting or accepting payments outside the app is a serious violation.

## 3. The Renter Code
As a Renter, you are a guest in someone else's vehicle. 
*   **Treat the Car as Your Own**: Be careful with the interior and exterior.
*   **Be On Time**: Respect the Host's schedule for handovers and returns.
*   **Communicate**: If you're running late or something goes wrong, notify the Host immediately.
*   **Return Clean**: Remove your trash and leave the car as clean as you found it.

## 4. The Host Code
As a Host, you are providing a vital service to the community.
*   **Safety First**: Never list a vehicle with known safety issues.
*   **Accuracy**: Ensure your listing photos and descriptions are current and honest.
*   **Reliability**: Minimize cancellations. Every cancellation disrupts a Renter's travel plans.
*   **Hospitality**: Provide a welcoming experience and a clean, fueled vehicle.

## 5. Review Integrity
Reviews are the lifeblood of our community's trust system.
*   **Be Honest**: Provide fair and objective feedback based on your actual experience.
*   **No Extortion**: Do not use the threat of a negative review to extract payments or concessions.
*   **No Bias**: Do not post reviews for your own listings or for competitors.

## 6. Prohibited Behavior
The following behaviors are strictly prohibited and will lead to account suspension or termination:
*   Fraudulent activity or identity theft.
*   Endangering public safety through reckless driving.
*   Using the Platform for any criminal activity.
*   Creating multiple accounts to bypass restrictions.
*   Spamming other Users with unsolicited offers.

## 7. Enforcement
We take these guidelines seriously. We use a combination of automated systems and manual reviews to identify and address violations. 
*   **Warnings**: For minor first-time violations.
*   **Suspension**: Temporary removal of platform access.
*   **Banning**: Permanent removal from the MobiRides community.

## 8. Reporting Violations
If you witness or experience a violation of these guidelines, please report it immediately through the "Report User" feature in the app or by emailing [community@app.mobirides.com](mailto:community@app.mobirides.com).
