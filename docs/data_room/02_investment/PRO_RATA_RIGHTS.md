# Pro-Rata Rights Documentation

**Document:** PRO-RATA-2026-001  
**Effective Date:** [DATE]

---

## 1. Overview

This document establishes pro-rata rights for investors in MobiRides pre-seed financing.

### Definition
**Pro-Rata Right** - The right to invest in future financing rounds to maintain ownership percentage.

---

## 2. Eligibility

Pro-Rata Rights apply to investors who:
- [ ] Invested ≥ P50,000 in pre-seed round
- [ ] Executed SAFE with Pro-Rata clause

---

## 3. Exercise Terms

### Timing
- Minimum 14 days notice before round closes
- Match terms offered to new investors

### Allocation
```
Pro-Rata Amount = (Investor Ownership %) × (New Round Investment)

New Investor Amount = (Pro-Rata Amount) + (Additional Investment)
```

---

## 4. Limits

### Maximum Pro-Rata Investment
- Up to 2x original investment amount
- At company's discretion to increase

### Waiver
- Investor may waive pro-rata rights
- Must provide written notice

---

## 5. Process

### Step 1: Notice
Company notifies investors 30 days before financing round.

### Step 2: Election
Investors have 14 days to exercise pro-rata.

### Step 3: Documentation
Investor signs new SAFEs within 7 days.

### Step 4: Closing
Funds due within 5 business days.

---

## 6. Exceptions

### Dilution from Option Pool
Pro-rata calculated AFTER option pool expansion.

### Down Round
If valuation < pre-money, pro-rata rights still apply but at new valuation.

---

## 7. Transfer

Pro-Rata rights are:
- [ ] Not transferable without company consent
- [ ] Pass to estate/advisor only

---

## 8. Company Undertaking

- [ ] Notify investors of future rounds
- [ ] Provide term sheets 30 days in advance
- [ ] Honor pro-rata calculations
