## 1. Host Eligibility and Responsibilities
### 1.1 Ownership and Authority
You represent and warrant that you are the legal owner of the vehicle or have the express written authority from the owner to list the vehicle on the Platform. The vehicle must be registered, licensed, and roadworthy in accordance with the **Road Traffic Act of Botswana**.

### 1.2 Accuracy of Listings
You must provide accurate and complete information about your vehicle, including its condition, features, and availability. You are responsible for keeping your Listing up to date.

### 1.3 Vehicle Maintenance
You are responsible for maintaining your vehicle in a safe and reliable condition. This includes regular servicing, tire maintenance, and ensuring all safety features (e.g., brakes, lights, seatbelts) are fully functional. MobiRides reserves the right to suspend any listing that does not meet our safety standards.

## 2. Insurance and Liability
### 2.1 Coverage
All vehicles listed on MobiRides must be covered by a valid comprehensive insurance policy. While MobiRides may facilitate supplemental insurance through our partners, the primary responsibility for maintaining basic legal insurance rests with the Host.

### 2.2 Reporting Damages
Hosts must inspect their vehicle immediately upon return. Any damages incurred during a rental period must be reported to MobiRides within **24 hours** of the booking's end time. Failure to report within this window may void your ability to claim compensation or insurance coverage.

## 3. Financial Terms
### 3.1 Host Earnings
The earnings for each booking will be calculated based on the daily rate set by the Host, minus the MobiRides platform fee.

### 3.2 Payout Schedule
Payouts for completed bookings are processed on a **T+2 business day** cycle (two business days after the successful completion and return of the vehicle). Funds will be transferred to the bank account provided in your Host Profile.

### 3.3 Taxes
Hosts are solely responsible for declaring and paying any applicable taxes (including VAT where applicable) on the income generated through the Platform.

## 4. Booking Management
### 4.1 Cancellations
Hosts are encouraged to honor all confirmed bookings. Frequent cancellations by a Host may result in penalties, including reduced visibility in search results or account suspension. 

### 4.2 Handover and Return
Hosts must be present (or provide clear instructions for self-check-in) for the vehicle handover. You must verify the Renter’s identity and Driver’s License at the start of the trip.

## 5. Termination
MobiRides reserves the right to terminate or suspend your Host account at any time for violations of these terms, poor vehicle maintenance, or a history of low user ratings.
