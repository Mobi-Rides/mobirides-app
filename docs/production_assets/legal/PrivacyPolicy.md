# MobiRides Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** May 8, 2026  
**Version:** 1.2

MobiRides ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information in accordance with the **Data Protection Act [Cap 08:05] (Act No. 32 of 2018)** of Botswana and applicable international data protection standards.

---

## 1. Data Controller and Registration
MobiRides operates as the **Data Controller** for the personal information collected through our website, mobile application, and services (the "Platform"). We are committed to maintaining the principles of accountability, lawfulness, and transparency as mandated by the Act.

## 2. Definitions
*   **"Personal Data"**: Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person (data subject).
*   **"Sensitive Personal Data"**: Personal data revealing religious, philosophical, or political beliefs, health, sexual life, or **biometric data** (Section 2 of the Act).
*   **"Processing"**: Any operation performed on personal data, such as collection, storage, use, or disclosure.

## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Personal Data
*   **Account Details**: Name, email address, phone number, and password.
*   **Identity Information**: National ID/Passport number, Driver's License details.
*   **Financial Data**: Bank account details for Hosts; payment tokenization for Renters (MobiRides does not store full credit card numbers).

### 3.2 Sensitive Personal Data (Biometrics)
To ensure the safety of our community, we process biometric data:
*   **Identity Verification**: We collect photographs of your Identity Documents and "selfies" to perform facial matching against official records. 
*   **Requirement for Consent**: By using our identity verification services, you provide **explicit consent** for the processing of this sensitive data as per Section 24 of the Act.

### 3.3 Automatically Collected Data
*   **Location Data**: Real-time GPS coordinates to facilitate vehicle discovery and booking tracking.
*   **Usage Metadata**: IP addresses, device identifiers, and platform interaction logs.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing
In accordance with Sections 17-21 of the Act, we process data based on:
1.  **Consent**: Explicit permission provided by you.
2.  **Contractual Necessity**: To execute the rental agreement between you and another User.
3.  **Legal Obligation**: To comply with KYC (Know Your Customer) and AML (Anti-Money Laundering) requirements.
4.  **Legitimate Interests**: To protect platform integrity and user safety.

## 5. Purpose of Processing
We use your data to:
*   Verify your identity and eligibility to drive.
*   Facilitate secure payments and bookings.
*   Provide customer support and resolve disputes.
*   Prevent fraudulent activity and unauthorized access.
*   Comply with legal requests from the Botswana Police Service or other regulatory bodies.

## 6. International Data Transfers
MobiRides utilizes global infrastructure providers (including servers located in South Africa, Europe, and North America). 
*   **Safeguards**: In accordance with **Section 48** of the Data Protection Act, we ensure that any transfer of personal data outside Botswana is protected by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or occurs to jurisdictions providing an adequate level of protection as recognized by the Commissioner.

## 7. Data Security and Breach Notification
We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure.
*   **Notification**: In the event of a data breach that poses a high risk to your rights and freedoms, MobiRides will notify the **Information and Data Protection Commissioner** and affected data subjects within 72 hours of discovery, as per Section 34 of the Act.

## 8. Data Retention
We retain personal data only for as long as necessary:
*   **KYC Records**: Retained for the duration of your account plus a mandatory 5-year period to comply with financial regulations.
*   **Messaging**: In-app communications are stored for the duration of the associated booking lifecycle for dispute resolution.
*   **Deletion**: Upon account termination, data is anonymized or deleted within 90 days, unless a legal hold is in place.

## 9. Your Statutory Rights
Under Part VI (Sections 38–44) of the Act, you have the right to:
1.  **Right of Access**: Request a copy of all data held about you.
2.  **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
3.  **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion of data where processing is no longer justified.
4.  **Right to Object**: Object to processing for direct marketing or based on legitimate interests.
5.  **Right to Restriction**: Limit how your data is used during a dispute.

To exercise these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at **privacy@app.mobirides.com**.

## 10. Regulatory Recourse
If you believe your data has been handled in violation of the Act, you have the right to lodge a complaint with:
**The Information and Data Protection Commissioner**  
Private Bag 001, Gaborone, Botswana.
