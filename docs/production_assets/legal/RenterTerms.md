## 1. Renter Eligibility and Verification
### 1.1 Minimum Age and Experience
To rent a vehicle on MobiRides, you must be at least **21 years of age** and have held a valid, unencumbered driver’s license for a minimum of **two (2) years**. 

### 1.2 Identity Verification (KYC)
You must complete the MobiRides verification process, which includes providing a clear photograph of your National ID or Passport, a valid Driver’s License, and a "selfie" for biometric matching. You authorize MobiRides to perform background checks on your driving record where applicable.

### 1.3 Account Security
You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials. You must not allow any other person to drive a vehicle booked through your account unless they are registered as an "Additional Driver" on the platform.

## 2. Prohibited Uses
The vehicle must NOT be used for any of the following:
*   **Off-Roading**: Driving on unpaved roads, tracks, or terrain not designated for regular vehicle traffic (unless specifically authorized in the vehicle listing).
*   **Commercial Use**: Using the vehicle for ride-hailing (e.g., taxi), courier services, or any other commercial purpose without express written consent.
*   **Racing and Speeding**: Engaging in racing, speed testing, or stunts.
*   **Sub-Leasing**: Renting the vehicle out to third parties.
*   **Illegal Activity**: Transporting illegal substances, weapons, or using the vehicle to commit a crime.
*   **Hazardous Materials**: Transporting flammable, explosive, or toxic materials.

## 3. Renter Responsibilities
### 3.1 Vehicle Care
You must treat the vehicle with care and return it in the same condition in which it was received, excluding normal wear and tear.

### 3.2 Fuel and Cleanliness
You must return the vehicle with the same fuel level as documented at the start of the trip. If the vehicle is returned with less fuel, you will be charged for the missing fuel plus a service fee. You must also return the vehicle in a clean state; excessive dirt, odors, or pet hair may result in a cleaning fee.

### 3.3 Smoking Prohibited
Smoking of any substance (including e-cigarettes and vaping) is strictly prohibited in all MobiRides vehicles. A cleaning and decontamination fee will be charged for any violations.

Vehicles must be returned by the agreed-upon time. Late returns without prior authorization will result in an hourly late fee or a full day's rental charge plus a penalty.

2.  **Notify MobiRides** via the app or support line within **two (2) hours** of the incident.
