# Help Center Guide: Refer a Friend & Earn Rewards
**Last updated:** May 8, 2026
**Role:** Shared (Renter & Host)

Community is at the heart of MobiRides. Our referral program rewards you for bringing trusted friends and family into our network.

---

## 1. How the Program Works
It’s simple: you share your love for MobiRides, and we reward you both.
*   **Your Code**: Find your unique referral code in your Profile under the "Refer a Friend" section.
*   **Sharing**: Share your code via WhatsApp, SMS, Email, or Social Media.
*   **The Friend's Reward**: When your friend signs up with your code, they receive an instant **P50 MobiRides Discount** on their first booking.

## 2. Your Rewards
You earn a **P100 MobiCredit** for every successful referral.
*   **Successful Referral (Renter)**: Defined as when your friend completes their first trip and the trip is marked as "Completed."
*   **Successful Referral (Host)**: Defined as when your friend lists their first vehicle and completes their first booking as a host.
*   **Payout**: Your MobiCredit is added to your MobiWallet within 24 hours of the successful referral event.

## 3. How to Use Your MobiCredit
MobiCredit is flexible and easy to use.
*   **For Renters**: Use your credit to discount your next rental. Just select "Apply MobiWallet Balance" during checkout.
*   **For Hosts**: You can use your credit for rentals or withdraw it to your bank account along with your regular earnings.
*   **Expiration**: MobiCredits are valid for **12 months** from the date of issue.

## 4. Referral Program Rules
To keep the community fair, we have a few rules:
*   **New Users Only**: Referral codes only work for people who have never had a MobiRides account.
*   **Self-Referral**: You cannot refer yourself or create multiple accounts.
*   **Fair Play**: We monitor for fraudulent activity. Misuse of the referral program will result in the loss of credits and potential account suspension.
