# Help Center Guide: Community Guidelines & Conduct
**Last updated:** May 8, 2026
**Role:** Shared (Renter & Host)

MobiRides is built on trust, respect, and mutual responsibility. Our community guidelines ensure that every member has a positive and safe experience.

---

## 1. Respect & Professionalism
MobiRides is a diverse community. We have zero tolerance for discrimination, harassment, or abusive behaviour.
*   **Communication**: Always use the in-app messaging system. Be clear, polite, and responsive.
*   **Privacy**: Never share another user's personal contact details, address, or ID outside the context of a confirmed booking.
*   **Inclusivity**: We celebrate the diversity of our users. Any discrimination based on race, gender, religion, or background will result in an immediate permanent ban.

## 2. Vehicle Stewardship
Treat every car like it’s your own (or better!).
*   **Cleanliness**: Renters should return cars in the same state of cleanliness they were received.
*   **Smoking**: Smoking is strictly prohibited in all MobiRides vehicles. This includes Vaping and E-cigarettes.
*   **Pets**: Check the listing before bringing a pet. If allowed, ensure they are in a carrier or use a seat cover to prevent hair and odours.
*   **Fuel**: Always return the vehicle with the agreed-upon fuel level (Full-to-Full is the standard).

## 3. Honest & Integrity
The marketplace only works if everyone is truthful.
*   **Listings**: Hosts must provide accurate photos and descriptions. Using stock photos or misrepresenting features is a violation of our terms.
*   **Reviews**: Leave honest, objective feedback. Never offer or accept incentives (like discounts) in exchange for a positive review.
*   **Reporting**: If something goes wrong, report it immediately and truthfully. Covering up damage or mechanical issues puts the next user at risk.

## 4. Safety First
Your safety and the safety of the public are our top priorities.
*   **Traffic Laws**: Obey all Botswana road traffic regulations, speed limits, and parking rules.
*   **Unauthorized Drivers**: Never let anyone else drive a vehicle you have booked. Only verified MobiRides users are covered by our insurance.
*   **Maintenance**: Hosts must keep their vehicles in peak mechanical condition. If a car feels unsafe at pickup, Renters should not start the trip and should notify MobiRides immediately.

---
**Seen something that doesn't feel right?** Use the "Report" feature in the app or contact our 24/7 Safety Team via the Help Center.
