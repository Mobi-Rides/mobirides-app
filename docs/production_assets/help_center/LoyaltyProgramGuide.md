# Help Center Guide: Mobi Rewards Loyalty Program
**Last updated:** May 8, 2026
**Role:** Shared (Renter & Host)

Every kilometer you travel and every booking you complete brings you closer to exclusive rewards. Mobi Rewards is our way of saying thank you for being part of our journey.

---

## 1. Membership Tiers
Our program features three levels of benefits. Your tier is based on your activity over the last 6 months.

### **Blue Tier (All New Users)**
*   **Benefits**: Access to all platform features, standard support, and basic insurance coverage.
*   **Requirements**: Sign up and verify your account.

### **Gold Tier**
*   **Benefits**: 5% discount on all service fees, priority customer support, and reduced insurance excess rates.
*   **Requirements**: Complete 10 trips (Renters) or maintain 3 active listings (Hosts) with a rating of 4.7+.

### **Platinum Tier**
*   **Benefits**: 10% discount on all service fees, a dedicated account manager, lowest insurance excess rates, and early access to new platform features.
*   **Requirements**: Complete 25 trips (Renters) or maintain 5+ active listings (Hosts) with a rating of 4.9+.

## 2. Earning Mobi Points
Points are the currency of Mobi Rewards.
*   **Renters**: Earn **1 Point for every P10** spent on rentals.
*   **Hosts**: Earn **1 Point for every P50** earned in revenue.
*   **Bonuses**: Earn extra points for completing "Safety Challenges" or leaving detailed reviews with photos.

## 3. Redeeming Your Points
You can spend your points in the Rewards Store:
*   **Rental Discounts**: 100 Points = P10 off your next trip.
*   **Protection Upgrades**: Use points to upgrade your insurance plan for free.
*   **Vouchers**: Redeem points for vouchers from our Botswana-based partner brands (fuel, dining, and travel).

## 4. Keeping Your Tier
We review tier status twice a year (January and July).
*   **Maintain Your Activity**: If your activity falls below your tier's threshold, you will be moved to the previous tier at the next review date.
*   **Rating Protection**: We understand one bad review shouldn't ruin your status. We exclude your lowest review from the tier calculation to protect our best users.

---
