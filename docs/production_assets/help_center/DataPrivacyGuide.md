# Help Center Guide: Your Data Privacy & Protection

**Last updated:** May 8, 2026
**Category:** Security & Privacy
**Role:** Shared (Renter & Host)

At MobiRides, we believe your data belongs to you. This guide explains how we handle your personal information in accordance with the **Botswana Data Protection Act [Cap 08:05] (Act No. 32 of 2018)**.

---

## 1. Our Commitment to Privacy
MobiRides is designed with "Privacy by Design" principles. We ensure that every feature we build respects your rights as a data subject and adheres to the statutory requirements of Botswana law.

## 2. What Data We Collect & Why
We only collect data that is strictly necessary to provide a safe and reliable service.
*   **Identity & KYC**: We verify your ID/Passport and Driver's License to ensure platform security. This includes biometric matching (facial recognition) to prevent identity theft.
*   **Location Services**: We use real-time location data to help you find vehicles and to facilitate check-in/check-out.
*   **Payment Security**: Payments are handled by PCI-DSS compliant gateways. MobiRides does not store your full card details on our servers.

## 3. Your Statutory Rights
Under Botswana law, you have powerful rights over your personal data:
*   **Access & Portability**: You can request a copy of your personal data at any time.
*   **Rectification**: You have the right to correct inaccurate data.
*   **Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")**: You can request the deletion of your account and data, subject to legal retention periods (typically 5 years for financial and KYC records).
*   **Objection & Restriction**: You can object to specific uses of your data, such as direct marketing.

## 4. International Data Protection
While our primary operations are in Botswana, we use global cloud infrastructure to ensure high availability.
*   **Safeguards**: All data transferred outside Botswana is protected by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) to ensure a level of protection consistent with the Botswana DPA.

## 5. Reporting a Privacy Concern
If you suspect your account has been compromised or have concerns about how your data is being handled:
1.  **Change your password** immediately.
2.  **Contact our Data Protection Officer** at privacy@app.mobirides.com.
3.  **Regulatory Recourse**: You always have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information and Data Protection Commissioner of Botswana.
