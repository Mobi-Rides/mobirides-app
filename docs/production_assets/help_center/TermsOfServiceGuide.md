# Help Center Guide: Understanding Our Terms of Service
**Last updated:** May 8, 2026
**Role:** Shared (Renter & Host)

Welcome to the MobiRides community. Our Terms of Service are designed to ensure a safe, fair, and transparent marketplace for vehicle sharing in Botswana. This guide breaks down the core elements of our legal agreement in plain language.

---

## 1. Account Ownership & Security
Your MobiRides account is personal and non-transferable.
*   **Verification**: All users must complete identity verification. This includes providing a valid National ID or Passport and a Driver's License recognized by the Botswana DRTS.
*   **Confidentiality**: You are responsible for all activity on your account. Never share your password or allow others to use your account to book or list vehicles.
*   **Accuracy**: You must keep your contact information and documentation up to date. Expired licenses will result in automatic booking suspension.

## 2. The Nature of the Marketplace
It is important to understand that **MobiRides is a platform, not a rental company.**
*   **The Contract**: When a booking is confirmed, a legal contract is formed directly between the Host and the Renter.
*   **Our Role**: MobiRides facilitates the platform, processes payments, provides mediation services, and coordinates insurance coverage. We do not own the vehicles listed on the platform.

## 3. Financial Transparency
We believe in clear, upfront pricing with no hidden fees.
*   **Booking Fees**: Renters pay a service fee to cover platform operations and 24/7 support.
*   **Host Commission**: MobiRides deducts a percentage (typically 15%) from the Host's earnings to maintain the marketplace and process payments.
*   **Security Deposits**: Some bookings may require a security deposit, which is held by MobiRides and released 48 hours after the successful return of the vehicle.

## 4. Dispute Resolution & Mediation
Disputes are rare, but we have a robust process to handle them fairly.
*   **First Step**: We always encourage Users to communicate directly via the in-app chat to resolve minor issues.
*   **Platform Intervention**: If an agreement cannot be reached, either party can "Escalate to Support."
*   **Evidence-Based Decisions**: Our mediation team reviews all available evidence, including chat logs, handover photos, and vehicle tracking data, to make a binding decision.

## 5. Community Safety & Term Violations
We maintain high standards to keep our community safe.
*   **Zero Tolerance**: Illegal activities, reckless driving, and harassment result in immediate and permanent account bans.
*   **Reporting**: If you encounter a user violating our terms, use the "Report User" button on their profile or within your booking screen.
