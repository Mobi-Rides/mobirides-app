# Help Center Guide: The Perfect Vehicle Handover
**Last updated:** May 8, 2026
**Role:** Host

A smooth handover sets the tone for the entire rental and protects you from potential disputes. Follow this step-by-step guide to ensure every trip starts right.

---

## 1. Preparing for the Renter
Your car should be "Showroom Ready."
*   **Cleanliness**: A clean car earns 5-star reviews. Ensure the interior is vacuumed and the exterior is washed.
*   **Maintenance**: Check tyre pressure, oil, and coolant levels.
*   **Fuel**: Provide the car with a full tank. This makes it easy for the renter to return it full.
*   **Personal Items**: Remove all personal belongings. Ensure the insurance disc is clearly displayed.

## 2. The Identity Check (Crucial!)
Never hand over keys without verifying the driver.
*   **Physical License**: Ask to see the Renter's physical driver's license.
*   **Match the Profile**: Verify that the name and face on the license match the Renter's MobiRides profile.
*   **Vehicle Class**: Ensure their license is valid for your vehicle's class (e.g., Class B for light vehicles).

*If the person picking up the car is not the verified Renter, do not provide the keys and contact support immediately.*

## 3. Using the In-App Handover Checklist
Our app guides you through a mandatory 5-step process:
1.  **Mileage & Fuel**: Record the current odometer reading and fuel level. Take a photo of the dashboard.
2.  **Exterior Photos**: Take at least 4 photos (front, back, left side, right side). Zoom in on any existing scratches or dents.
3.  **Interior Photos**: Take photos of the seats, floor mats, and any special features.
4.  **Key Exchange**: Demonstrate any "quirks" your car has (e.g., how to open the fuel cap, use the infotainment, or engage 4WD).
5.  **Digital Signature**: Both you and the Renter must tap "Confirm Handover" in the app.

## 4. Setting Expectations
Spend 5 minutes talking to your Renter:
*   **Return Location**: Confirm where and how the car should be returned.
*   **Emergency Contact**: Remind them how to reach you via the app.
*   **House Rules**: Briefly mention your preferences regarding pets or cleaning.

## 5. Completing the Trip
When the car is returned:
*   **Inspect Immediately**: Perform a walkaround with the Renter if possible.
*   **Return Checklist**: Complete the return checklist in the app to record final mileage and fuel.
*   **Leave a Review**: A quick, honest review helps the whole community.
