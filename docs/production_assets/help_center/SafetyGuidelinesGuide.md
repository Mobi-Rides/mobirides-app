# Help Center Guide: Renter Safety & Emergency Procedures
**Last updated:** May 8, 2026
**Role:** Renter

Your safety is our absolute priority. This guide provides essential information for a safe driving experience and clear instructions on what to do if the unexpected happens.

---

## 1. Before You Drive: The 2-Minute Safety Check
Never start a trip without performing these basic checks:
*   **The Walkaround**: Check for any unreported damage, tyre condition, and fluid leaks.
*   **Lights & Signals**: Ensure headlights, brake lights, and indicators are working.
*   **Interior Basics**: Adjust your mirrors, test the brakes, and ensure all seatbelts are functional.
*   **Documentation**: Verify that the insurance disc and registration documents are present in the vehicle.

*If you find a serious safety issue, do not start the trip. Report it in the app immediately.*

## 2. Safe Driving in Botswana
*   **Stay Alert**: Botswana has unique road challenges, including livestock on roads and varying road conditions. Avoid driving at night on rural roads.
*   **Speed Limits**: Strictly adhere to the 60km/h (urban) and 120km/h (highway) limits. Many vehicles on MobiRides are equipped with telematics that alert us to excessive speeding.
*   **Zero Tolerance**: Never drink and drive. A single violation of this rule results in a permanent platform ban and loss of insurance coverage.

## 3. What to Do in an Accident
1.  **Check for Injuries**: Call **997 (Ambulance)** or **999 (Police)** immediately if anyone is hurt.
2.  **Ensure Safety**: If possible, move the vehicle to a safe spot and turn on hazard lights.
3.  **Exchange Information**: Get the name, phone number, vehicle registration, and insurance details of any other parties involved.
4.  **Document the Scene**: Take clear photos of all vehicles involved, the surrounding area, and any road markings or signs.
5.  **Police Report**: In Botswana, you must report all accidents to the police within 24 hours.

## 4. Reporting to MobiRides
*   **The 12-Hour Rule**: All incidents must be reported to MobiRides via the "Report Issue" button in your active booking screen within 12 hours.
*   **Insurance Coordination**: Our claims team will contact you once the report is filed to guide you through the process.

## 5. Mechanical Breakdowns
*   **Roadside Assistance**: MobiRides provides 24/7 roadside assistance. You can find the contact number in your booking confirmation email and the "Active Trip" screen.
*   **Communication**: Notify your Host immediately via the in-app chat. They may be able to provide specific guidance for their vehicle.
*   **Unauthorized Repairs**: Never authorize or attempt repairs yourself. All maintenance must be coordinated through MobiRides to ensure safety and insurance compliance.

---
**Safe travels!** Remember, our support team is available 24/7 via the app for any safety concerns.
