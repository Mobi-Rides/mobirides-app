# Help Center Guide: Cancellation & Refund Policy
**Last updated:** May 8, 2026
**Role:** Shared (Renter & Host)

Plans change. We've designed our cancellation policy to balance flexibility for renters with predictability for hosts. This guide explains how cancellations work and how refunds are calculated.

---

## 1. Renter Cancellation Policy
Your refund amount depends on how far in advance you cancel.

*   **Free Cancellation (Full Refund)**: You can cancel any booking for a full refund up to **48 hours** before the scheduled start time.
*   **Partial Refund (75%)**: Cancellations made between **24 and 48 hours** before the start time will receive a 75% refund of the daily rate (fees may be non-refundable).
*   **Last-Minute Cancellation (50%)**: Cancellations made within **24 hours** of the start time will receive a 50% refund. This compensates the host for the lost booking opportunity.
*   **No-Show**: If you do not collect the vehicle within 2 hours of the start time and haven't communicated with the host, the booking is marked as a No-Show and no refund is issued.

## 2. Host Cancellation Policy
Hosts should only cancel under extreme circumstances.
*   **Renter Protection**: If a Host cancels, the Renter always receives a **100% refund**, including all fees.
*   **Host Penalties**: Automated cancellations by hosts may result in a cancellation fee, blocked calendar dates, and a "Cancelled Booking" note on their profile.
*   **Repeated Cancellations**: Hosts who cancel 3 or more bookings in a 6-month period will have their account reviewed for potential suspension.

## 3. How to Request a Refund
Refunds are initiated automatically when a cancellation is processed through the app.
*   **Original Payment Method**: Refunds are always sent back to the card or wallet used for the initial booking.
*   **MobiWallet**: You can choose to receive your refund as MobiWallet credit for instant access.
*   **Processing Time**: While we initiate refunds immediately, it can take 5-10 business days for your bank to reflect the transaction.

## 4. Exceptional Circumstances (Extenuating Circumstances)
We understand that sometimes life happens. You may be eligible for a full refund outside the standard policy for:
*   **Documented Medical Emergencies**: Serious illness or injury.
*   **Government Restrictions**: Sudden travel bans or lockdowns.
*   **Natural Disasters**: Severe weather events preventing travel.
*   **Safety Issues**: The vehicle is found to be unsafe or significantly misrepresented at pickup.

*To claim an exception, you must provide supporting documentation to our support team within 24 hours of cancellation.*
