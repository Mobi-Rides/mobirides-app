## Executive Summary

This roadmap guides MobiRides from its current 86% production readiness to full commercial launch and growth across H1 2026. The platform is live with 247 users and 66 vehicles. Critical work remaining centres on **security remediation (MOB-700)**, **payment provider integration (DPO/Paygate + Ooze)**, **BUG-003/BUG-006 resolution**, and **test coverage uplift** before scaling into market expansion and the Rent2Buy pilot.

**Key Themes:**
- Security remediation & compliance (MOB-700, Botswana DPA)
- Payment system production deployment (DPO/Paygate, Ooze Mobile Money)
- Pay-U Damage Protection SLA v1.1 completion
- Rent2Buy marketplace pilot (Q2 2026)
- Pre-seed funding close (P700K target)

**Year 1 Revenue Target:** P1.368M (P60K → P157.5K/month trajectory, +264% growth)
