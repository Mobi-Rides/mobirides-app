## Executive Summary

This roadmap guides MobiRides from its current 95% production readiness to the **V1.0 Official Commercial Launch** and growth across H1 2026. The platform is live with 317 users and 66 vehicles. We have successfully completed **Payment Phase 0**, **Booking UX modernization**, and **Map Navigation remediation**. Critical work remaining centers on **SuperAdmin security hardening** and **Native Push Notification provisioning** (MOB-13).

**Key Themes:**
- Security remediation & compliance (MOB-700, Botswana DPA)
- Payment system production deployment (DPO/Paygate, Ooze Mobile Money)
- Pay-U Damage Protection SLA v1.1 completion
- Rent2Buy marketplace pilot (Q2 2026)
- Pre-seed funding close (P700K target)

**Year 1 Revenue Target:** P1.368M (P60K → P157.5K/month trajectory, +264% growth)
